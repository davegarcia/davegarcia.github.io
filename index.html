About Me

Dave Garcia

Managing Director

I have over 39 years experience, working with municipalities, corporations, news stations, and other companies to craft their brand identity. I thrive on understanding the current media landscape and conveying that knowledge to my clients. The business and media environment can change overnight. The only way to succeed is to build your business and it’s communication strategy on sound principals, understood by the right people. I focus on providing the training and tools to protect your business even before a crisis begins and to seize opportunities as soon they arise.
